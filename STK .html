About the Game

Survive the Killer is a thrilling multiplayer game where you either play as the killer or a survivor. As a survivor, your goal is to escape before the killer gets you. As the killer, your job is to eliminate the survivors before they escape!

Tips & Tricks

  • As a survivor: Stick with teammates and look for exits early.
  • As the killer: Use the element of surprise and block exits strategically.
  • Keep an eye on the clock and always have an escape plan.
